About Career Buddy

Career Buddy is a chatbot created by Neeli Tejaswani, Shaik Shajineen, and Shaik Sofiya Asmi, third-year BTech students from SSITS College. It helps you understand different software jobs like Front-End Developer, Back-End Developer, Cybersecurity Analyst, Cloud Engineer, UI/UX Designer, etc.

It shows salary ranges, job demand, and how to start in these fields. Career Buddy also offers little information on resumes, portfolios, courses, and LinkedIn. “Your guide to a successful career in the software field!”
